We are HAZH Quad from the Faculty of Applied Science, Department of Computing at Rajarata University. Under the supervision of Ms. Piyumi Herath, we are conducting a survey for educational purposes as part of our project focused on Applying HCI principles to design and develop an interactive web application for the faculty.

In this survey, we aim to gather insights into user needs and preferences to guide the development of a web application that effectively applies HCI principles. Your feedback will help us create an intuitive and user-friendly interface tailored to the needs of faculty members and students.
